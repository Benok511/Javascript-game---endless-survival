About the game

In this game you play as a Grand Wizard who is trapped in a purgatory of infinite size your goal is to survive as long as possible while waves of enemies come to kill you.

Every 5 rounds there is a Boss Known as the Overseer he is quite slow but you can never escape him as he has sight of you at all time

as the rounds progress there are more enemies and they get stronger and smarter.

How far will you get?

How to Play!

W A S D TO MOVE or you may also use arrow keys

you shoot with left click

to pick up a power up just run through it but be careful enemies can break them if you are not quick enough

to reload your staff just left click again after reaching 0
